What is a content strategist?

A content strategist is someone who has expertise in creating content. They start with the need to communicate, infuse it with meaning, and promote it through optimized channels.

A content strategist is a linguist, a data scientist, a product manager, a product marketer, and a community manager, all in one.

To begin to image what a strategist can do for you, update that old adage "content is king" to:

content is queen — think chess
